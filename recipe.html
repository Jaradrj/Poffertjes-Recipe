Recipe

Poffertjes for 4 people

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs (size M)
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 packet of vanilla sugar
  • 50 g sugar
  • 250 g flour
  • 150 ml milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil
  • 75 g butter
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar

Recipe

1. Do a "Mise en place"

2. Beat eggs, salt, vanilla sugar, and sugar with a whisk

3. Gradually stir in 200 g of flour and milk.
Mix 50 g of flour with baking powder and fold into the batter

4. Grease the pan with butter or oil

5. Cook on low to medium heat until golden brown,
flipping when small bubbles form on the surface

Result

6. Serve with butter and powdered sugar
